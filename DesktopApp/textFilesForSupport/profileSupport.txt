Profile Page: Here you will see your username in it's encrypted format,
the current role of your account and the restaurant you account is assigned with.
This page can be used to check your user role. Having different user roles within this
fridge application will enable you to have different functionalities.

As a delivery person you can:
- Login.
- View the food items within the system.

As a chef you can:
- Login
- Request to add food to the database.
- Remove food from the database.
- View all existing food items and their information.
- Search for food items.

As a head Chef you can:
- Do all the same things as the chef.
- Generate food health reports.
- Add new users.
- Remove users.
- Change the role of a user.
